@answerlay/embed — CDN demo

This page demonstrates the embed widget loaded from the local Vite build, consuming fixture JSON in ./fixtures/. On a real site, you would point data-config-url at your published config.

Snippet

<script type="module" src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@answerlay/embed@1/dist/embed.js"></script>
<answerlay-chat
  data-assistant-id="demo"
  data-config-url="https://your-cdn.example.com/assistants/demo/config.json"
></answerlay-chat>

The widget renders in the bottom-right corner of the page.
